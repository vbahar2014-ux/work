⚡ Pattern of the Day
Yesterday the build-out went physical in Europe. Today it goes global — and every agent rollout ships with the governance plane bolted on.

The two threads of the week tightened in parallel. Capacity crossed into Asia — Meta's first India campus (168 MW at Jamnagar) — making sovereignty-and-power-driven siting a worldwide pattern, not a European one. Agents went population-scale again: KPMG's 276k Agent 365 landed within a week of the NHS's half-million seats, with Microsoft's Work IQ context APIs dated to GA on 16 June.

The sharper signal is what the vendors now sell: governance. Microsoft Agent 365, ServiceNow's govern-every-agent move and Okta's accountability-first framing all sell registration / identity / audit as the product — exactly as the EU converts the same idea into a hard 2-August obligation that has not moved (now ~51 days, Annex III deferral still a bet).

For Group Sovereign-first is now mainstream, not contrarian — a majority of enterprises have already acted. The single most consequential near-term call is which agent control plane to standardise on, because at population scale that choice sets Group's identity, audit and lock-in model.
