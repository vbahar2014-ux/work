⚡ Pattern of the Day
The sovereign-AI build-out leaves the slide deck and becomes steel, silicon & playbooks.

For weeks the story was abstraction — sovereignty becoming law, capacity becoming capital, agents becoming an obligation. Today it got physical. Europe's headline gigawatts turned into sited campuses (SoftBank/Sesterce 1 GW at Bosquel; Ardian/Verne 500 MW in Île-de-France); a complete non-Western stack matured in one keynote (Huawei Agentic Infra + Ascend 950DT + HYOK); the 800V power redesign got dedicated silicon (Skyworks at PCIM); and the EU turned its transparency principle into a published Article 50 Code of Practice. Meanwhile the agent contest narrowed to who owns the governance control plane.

For AG The sovereign-first posture now has specific decisions attached — which European landing zones, which agent control plane — against a 2-Aug clock that has not moved and a high-risk deferral that is still only a bet.
