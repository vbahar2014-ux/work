Tech & Sector Cloud Data Centers Networking AI & Agents Governance
⚡ Pattern of the Day
The governance gap is the real constraint — not the technology.
Across every domain today: agents exist but can't be governed at scale; cloud is multicloud-native but agent traffic has no observability; EU AI Act deadlines softened but the clock hasn't stopped; data centers have power and land but not regulatory clarity to use it freely; 6G promises sub-millisecond latency nobody can yet monetise. For AG, this is a single architectural challenge: build the agent and AI governance plane before the agents outrun it.
